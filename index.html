Desert Biome

Climate/Water Type

Deserts are Hot and Dry. Approximately 1 in. (2.5 cm) of rain falls in deserts per year. The average annual temperature of these miles of hot sand is 64° F (18° C). Deserts temperature ranges from 20 to 25° C.
